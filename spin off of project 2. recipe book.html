    • Spaghetti and Beans Mixture: This is one of our unique delicacy. very tasty and simple to make.
    Here are the table of ingredients required for a tasty and yummy dish of spag and beans mixture.

    1/2 Packet of Spagetti 1 Cup of Beans Soya Oil
    Fresh Pepper and tomatoes Onion Grinded Cray Fish
    Dry Fish Maggi Curry

Steps:

  1. wash the beans with water and add adequate water then place it on fire to cook for 15mins, then add the spagetti and allow to cook for additional 7mins
    after then, take off the fire, drain the remaining water and rinse the mixture with cold water.
  2. Wash and slice the tomatoes, pepper and onions
  3. Place 1/4 cup of soya oil on fire and allow to heat up for some seconds then pour the sliced veggies into it and allow to fry for say 3mins.
  4. then add small quantity of water and add the cleaned dry fish, seasonings and crayfish. allow the mixture to boil for say 3mins.
  5. then add the beans and spag into the mixture and cook till the water dries. you can add more water if need be.
  6. when the water is completely dry, turn the mixture and serve the yummy food. you can garnish it with boiled egg.

Yam and Red oil Sauce: The is one of the most common african delicacy mostly served in the rural areas. another very simple and fast dish to make.
Here are the required ingridients.

1 tuber of Yam Red Oil grinded pepper
salt Onion ukazi leave

Steps:

  1. Peel the yam tuber and dice into desired size.
  2. Wash with clean water and place in a pot
  3. Add some quantity of water, salt and place on fire too cook.
  4. Cook for like 10 to 15mins.Afterwards, drain the water.
  5. pour the desired quantity of red oil into a pan, slice the onion and add pepper and allow to fry for just few seconds.
